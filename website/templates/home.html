How it works

1. Set your profile
Set your semester to see your subjects (by sem: Maths-1, Data Structures, DBMS, Java), then choose progress level per subject and study hours per day.
2. Get matched
We suggest B.Tech study buddies whose progress matches or is slightly ahead of yours so you can learn together.
3. Take quizzes & chat
Take subject-wise daily quizzes and see solutions for wrong answers. Send a chat request from Match; when they accept, chat 1-on-1 on the Chat page.
4. Follow your schedule
Use the AI-suggested daily schedule based on your B.Tech subjects, level, and available hours.
