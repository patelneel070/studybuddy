How it works

Four steps for B.Tech students: find study buddies, take subject quizzes, and get an AI-suggested schedule.

1. Sign up and set your profile
Create an account, then select your semester (subjects are shown by semester), choose subjects and your progress level per subject (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced), and study hours per day.
2. Get matched with compatible buddies
We show study buddies who share at least one subject with you and have the same or better progress level in those subjects—so you can learn together effectively.
3. Send requests and chat
Send a request to a buddy. When they accept (simulated in the demo), they appear on your Requests and Chat pages. Open a 1-on-1 chat with any accepted buddy.
4. Follow your AI schedule
Take subject-wise daily quizzes (one per subject per day) and view your AI-suggested study schedule based on your B.Tech subjects, progress levels, and available hours.
