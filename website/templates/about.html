About StudyBuddy

Project Introduction

StudyBuddy is an AI-powered study partner matching platform for B.Tech students. It helps you find study buddies whose current progress matches or slightly exceeds yours, so you can learn together effectively.

Problem Statement

B.Tech students often struggle to find the right study partners—either too advanced or too behind. StudyBuddy solves this by matching on progress, enabling real-time chat, subject-wise quizzes, and suggesting a daily study schedule based on your profile.

Features
  • Secure sign up and login (simulated with localStorage)
  • Study profile: semester, subjects (by semester: Maths-1, Data Structures, DBMS, Java), progress level, study hours
  • Study buddy matching based on current progress
  • Subject-wise daily quiz (B.Tech subjects) with solutions for wrong answers
  • One-to-one chat with WebSocket-style simulation
  • AI-based daily study schedule suggestion
  • Responsive dark-themed UI with Bootstrap 5
Tech Stack

HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript, Bootstrap 5. Django backend (REST API, token auth); profile and auth use the API.

Purpose

StudyBuddy aims to make studying more effective and social by connecting students at similar levels and providing a clear, AI-suggested plan for how to use their study time each day.
